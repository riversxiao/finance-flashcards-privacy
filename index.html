Privacy Policy

Finance Flashcards

Last updated: January 2024

Introduction

Finance Flashcards ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

Information We Collect

Important: All data is stored locally on your device. We do not collect, transmit, or store any personal information on external servers.

The app stores the following information locally on your device:

  • Learning Progress: Your study progress, mastery levels, and review history
  • Bookmarked Terms: Terms you've saved for quick review
  • Settings: Your language preference and app settings
  • Game Progress: Your progress in educational games within the app

How We Use Information

All information collected is used solely for:

  • Providing and improving our educational services
  • Personalizing your learning experience
  • Saving your progress and preferences
  • Enabling offline functionality

Data Storage and Security

Local Storage Only: All data is stored exclusively on your device using local storage mechanisms (IndexedDB, localStorage). We do not have access to this data and it is never transmitted to our servers or third parties.

Third-Party Services

We do not share your data with third parties. The app does not contain:

  • Analytics or tracking services
  • Advertising networks
  • Data collection SDKs
  • Social media integrations that share data

Children's Privacy

Our app is educational and suitable for all ages (4+ rating). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. Since all data is stored locally on the device, no information is transmitted to us.

Your Rights

Since all data is stored locally on your device, you have complete control:

  • You can clear all data through the app's settings
  • You can delete the app to remove all stored data
  • You can use the app without creating an account

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy in the app and updating the "Last updated" date.

引言

财务卡片（"我们"、"我们的"或" ours"）致力于保护您的隐私。本隐私政策说明当您使用我们的移动应用时，我们如何收集、使用和保护您的信息。

我们收集的信息

重要提示： 所有数据都存储在您的设备本地。我们不会在外部服务器上收集、传输或存储任何个人信息。

应用会在您的设备本地存储以下信息：

  • 学习进度： 您的学习进度、掌握程度和复习历史
  • 收藏术语： 您保存以便快速复习的术语

信息使用方式

所有收集的信息仅用于：

  • 提供和改进我们的教育服务
  • 个性化您的学习体验
  • 保存您的进度和偏好
  • 支持离线功能

数据存储和安全

仅本地存储： 所有数据仅使用本地存储机制（IndexedDB、localStorage）存储在您的设备上。我们无法访问这些数据，也永远不会将其传输到我们的服务器或第三方。

第三方服务

我们不会与第三方共享您的数据。应用不包含：

  • 分析或跟踪服务
  • 广告网络
  • 数据收集 SDK
  • 共享数据的社交媒体集成

儿童隐私

我们的应用是教育性质的，适合所有年龄段（4+ 评级）。我们不会故意收集 13 岁以下儿童的个人信息。由于所有数据都存储在设备本地，不会有任何信息传输给我们。

您的权利

由于所有数据都存储在您的设备本地，您拥有完全的控制权：

  • 您可以通过应用设置清除所有数据
  • 您可以删除应用以移除所有存储的数据
  • 您可以在不创建账户的情况下使用应用

政策变更

我们可能会不时更新本隐私政策。我们会在应用中发布新的隐私政策并更新"最后更新"日期，以通知您任何变更。
